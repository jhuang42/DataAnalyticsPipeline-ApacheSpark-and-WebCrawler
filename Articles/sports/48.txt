You can expect lower rates between October and January, he said.</p>,class="css-1cy1v93 e2kc3sl0"><em class="css-2fg4z9 ehxkw330"><strong class="css-8qgvsz euv7paa0">Do prices surge on specific days or weeks of a month?</strong></em></p>,class="css-1cy1v93 e2kc3sl0">The stretch between the 26th and the 3rd of any given month is considered peak moving time, when availability is lowest and rates are highest across the industry, according to Mr. Katz.
